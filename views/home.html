Legend of Chima

The LEGO Legends of Chima series features mythic animal tribes, including raven and gorilla characters, in sets rich with action and lore. The intricate sets include detailed minifigures of ravens (corbeaux) and gorillas (gorilles), along with elaborate vehicles and structures, designed for immersive play and storytelling.

Star Wars: Build Your Own Adventure: Galactic Missions

"Star Wars: Build Your Own Adventure: Galactic Missions" is a LEGO and storybook combo that engages fans in immersive storytelling and building. It includes LEGO pieces and instructions to create models related to the Star Wars universe, encouraging readers to embark on their own intergalactic missions through imaginative play and construction.

Ninjago: Build Your Own Adventure

"Ninjago: Build Your Own Adventure" is part of the LEGO Build Your Own Adventure series that combines engaging storytelling with the creativity of LEGO building. This book set includes a Ninjago-themed story filled with exciting quests and challenges for the characters, along with LEGO bricks to build specific models related to the story.
